How Generative AI Image Generation Aligns with and Shapes Human Mental Images

This repository contains the public R analysis pipeline for a master thesis on GenAI image generation, mental imagery, iterative prompting, and image-agreement analysis.

Public-release rule: only scripts, documentation, the final anonymized dataset, and curated HTML/RTF/PNG research outputs belong in the public repository. Raw data, direct identifiers, intermediate datasets, private prompt-coding files, and participant-uploaded images are excluded.
